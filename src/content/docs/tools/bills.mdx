---
title: Bills
description: Look up bill payment categories and products.
---

Utility endpoints for discovering available bill payment options.

---

## Get Bill Categories

```http
GET /tools/bills/categories
```

Returns available bill categories (e.g., Airtime, Data, Utilities, Cable TV).

---

## List Bill Products

```http
GET /tools/bills/products
```

Returns all available bill products across categories.

---

## Get Bill Product

```http
GET /tools/bills/products/:id
```

Returns details for a single bill product including required inputs.
